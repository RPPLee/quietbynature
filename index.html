About

Quiet By Nature connects people with the natural world right in their own neighborhoods. We believe that a quick, quiet experience in nature can restore, inspire, and bring people together.

What We Do

Through our Neighborhood Nature program, we match experienced guides with guests for short, meaningful nature experiences — right in your local area. Whether it's a walk along a trail, sitting quietly in a meadow, or a facilitated conversation under the trees, we put nature and quiet on your schedule.

Our Values

  • Quiet activities in nature
  • Simple and rustic — reinforcing the beauty of Mother Earth
  • Respect and safety for all people and all nature
  • Inclusive and welcoming — all kind and respectful people are welcome
  • Every experience contributes to a nature-positive purpose

For Everyone

Our experiences are for anyone — families, children, caregivers, visitors, and anyone seeking a moment of peace outdoors. No experience necessary. Just a willingness to be present.
